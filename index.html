About Dr. Matthew Mayer

Matthew Mayer, MD, FAAP, FAAPMR is an Associate Professor in the Department of Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation at the University of Colorado School of Medicine and an attending physician in Pediatric Rehabilitation Medicine at Children’s Hospital Colorado. With over 15 years of experience in pediatric rehabilitation medicine, Dr. Mayer specializes in helping children with physical impairments achieve maximum development and independent function. His holistic, patient-centered approach has earned him high praise, including multiple patient satisfaction awards.

Through Pediatric Rehab Consulting, Dr. Mayer leverages his extensive clinical, academic, and leadership expertise to provide objective, evidence-based medical legal consulting for cases involving pediatric rehabilitation, trauma, and disability. His background ensures reliable testimony and consultations grounded in real-world medical practice. He emphasizes improving function and quality of life, guiding therapy recommendations, orthotics prescriptions, adaptive equipment, and comprehensive medical management.

Services

Pediatric Rehab Consulting offers tailored medical legal consulting services, drawing on Dr. Mayer’s deep expertise in pediatric rehabilitation to support attorneys, insurers, and organizations. Services include:

  • Expert Witness Testimony in pediatric rehabilitation, trauma, and disability cases
  • Independent Medical Examinations (IMEs)
  • Case File Reviews and Medical Record Analysis
  • Consultation on Treatment Plans, Prognosis, and Long-Term Outcomes

Available for cases nationwide, with a focus on ethical, unbiased assessments.
